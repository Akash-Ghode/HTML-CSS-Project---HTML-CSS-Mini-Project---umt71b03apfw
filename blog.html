Mumbai University provides full-time, open, and distance learning programs. The Institute of Distance and Open Learning [IDOL] was established to provide distance learning opportunities.   4. Master of Technology [M.Tech]
  5. Master of Engineering [M.E]
  6. Master of Arts [MA]

Entrance Exams Accepted by Mumbai University

Admission to most of the Mumbai University courses are based on entrance exams, while some are entirely on the basis of merit. The entrance examination accepted by Mumbai University are as follows.


  • JRF
  • SLET
  • MAH B.HMCT CET
  • MUCMET [Mumbai University Common Management Entrance Test]
  • MAH MBA
  • MMS CET
  • MAH MCA CET
  • MAH M.ARCH CET
  • GPAT
  • GATE
  • CSIR NET
  • UGC NET
  • MHT CET
