University of Mumbai

University of Mumbai, popularly known as Mumbai University, is one of the oldest and premier universities in India. It is recognised by the UGC and accredited with an ‘A++’ grade by the NAAC. Mumbai University has been ranked 45th among the top universities in India by NIRF Ranking 2022. MU has two main campuses- Vidyanagri & Fort and three sub-campuses at Ratnagiri, Thale, and Kalyan. The university has 56 Departments, 12 specialized Centres, 781+ Affiliated Colleges.

Our Global Campus

The Kalina campus, located in suburban Mumbai, is spread across 230 acres. The Fort campus is 13 acres. The campus has virtual classrooms, a library, lecture halls, etc.
