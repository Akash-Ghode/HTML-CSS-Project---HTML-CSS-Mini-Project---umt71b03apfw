MUMBAI UNIVERSITY OVERVIEW

Mumbai University was established in the year 1857 and was previously named the University of Bombay. It is the first state university in the country and is the oldest university in Maharashtra. The university is ranked 71 among the top universities in the country by NIRF. It has been accorded 5-star status and an A++ grade status by NAAC. The university offers various UG, PG, and doctoral programs under the departments of arts, commerce, science, technology, law, management, and arts. The University of Mumbai has a seven-point grading system.


Mumbai University has two main campuses. The university has 56 Departments and 12 specialized Centers. Mahatma Gandhi, Babasaheb Ambedkar, Mukesh Ambani, and Sunil Gavaskar are just a few of the renowned and well-known alumni of Mumbai University who have contributed to the nation throughout history. Mumbai University is dedicated to giving its students access to top-notch facilities, a variety of possibilities, and excellent guidance. Mumbai University also places a strong emphasis on a student's overall development.
